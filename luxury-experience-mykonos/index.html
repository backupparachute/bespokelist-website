What‚Äôs the Best Time to Go to Mykonos?

The best time to visit Mykonos depends on what you’re looking for on your vacation. Each season brings something different to the table, so make sure you use it to tailor your experience.

High Season (July and August):

  • Pros. This is when the island is most lively, with a bustling nightlife and numerous events. The weather is typically sunny and hot, perfect for beach activities.
  • Cons. Accommodations and flights can be significantly higher, and beaches and towns can get quite crowded.

Shoulder Season (May, June, September, and early October):

  • Pros. The weather is still warm and pleasant, but with fewer crowds than in high season. You’ll enjoy a more relaxed atmosphere, easier accommodation bookings, and often lower prices.
  • Cons. Some of the nightclubs and seasonal restaurants might start closing down in late September and October.

Off-Season (November to April):

  • Pros. You’ll find the island very quiet, with minimal tourists. It’s a good time to experience local life and enjoy the scenic beauty without the crowds.
  • Cons. Many hotels, restaurants, and shops may be closed, and the weather can be unpredictable, often cooler and windier, and not ideal for typical beach activities.

Overall, if you want a balance of good weather, active nightlife, and fewer crowds, late May, June, or September can be considered the best times to visit Mykonos.
